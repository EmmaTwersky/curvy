Curvy

by Emma Twersky

How to Play:

Select 'New Game' to begin.

Click on each hex tile to rotate it 60 degrees clockwise.

Winning:

All pathways on each hexagonal tile should be complete and lead to a similarly colored pathway on each adjacent tile.

About:

Never heard of Curvy before? That's a shame. The creators created a dynamic puzzle that deserves all of the credit behind this game.
